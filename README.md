Here's your 5-step wizard, styled just like the reference UI — light, clean, and guided. Here's what each step does:

Step 1 – Select Goal — radio options to pick the communication type (Incident Summary, Root Cause, Business Impact, EOD, etc.)

Step 2 – Pick Incident — incidents pulled from the MS Teams #cgx-incidents channel with a Teams banner. Click either incident to see its details fetched live from ServiceNow or Databricks via the two tabs below.

Step 3 – Set Context — audience role, output level, free-form notes, and toggles for human review, Databricks metrics, and auto EOD email.

Step 4 – AI Insights — animated progress screen showing the AI working through each step (fetch → correlate → draft) in real time before unlocking the next step.

Step 5 – Send Comm — channel selector cards (Teams Exec/IT/Business, Outlook EOD), message preview, and a Send button that confirms delivery.
